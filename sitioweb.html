THE REPUTATION ERA

Reputation is Taylor Swift's sixth studio album, marking a dramatic transformation in her musical style and public persona. Released on November 10, 2017, the album represents Swift's response to media scrutiny and her journey of self-discovery during a period of intense public attention.

With its darker tones and edgier production, Reputation explores themes of love found in the midst of chaos, the power of reputation, and the importance of trusting only those who truly know you. The album debuted at #1 on the Billboard 200 and spawned hit singles like "Look What You Made Me Do" and "Delicate."
